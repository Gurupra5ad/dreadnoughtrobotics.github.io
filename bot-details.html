Turret • Robosumo

Robo-sumo is a sport in which two robots attempt to push each other out of a ring. They may or may not have a weapon in the front and are divided into different weight categories. Points are provided based on the set of rules and regulations.






Skyline • Drone

Quadcopters are a category of drones that are controlled by four arms, and in each arm, there is a motor attached to a propeller. They can complete a given set of tasks and/or can overcome obstacles within a particular time. They are used for both racing and task-specific competitions.






Kaiser • Nitro Car

Nitro cars have engines powered with a fuel that contains some portion of nitromethane mixed with methanol. They are faster and provide a longer run time than electric cars. These cars are used for racing competitions in which they compete against each other.






Torpedo • All Terrain Vehicle

ATV is an RC controlled bot that can complete the arena by crossing the given obstacles successfully. Different obstacles and hurdles including bridges, speed breakers, marbles, etc. with checkpoints placed on the track for the bot to overcome and points are rewarded accordingly.






Mayhem • Drag Race

RC robots specially designed for racing with enhanced performance on speed are used for drag racing. In this race, bots compete against each other on a straight track to reach the end. Points are awarded based on the shortest time required to complete the track and penalties are provided according to a specific set of rules.
